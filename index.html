What to do

  1. Review the Idiom Flashcards until you know all 8.
  2. Take the Idiom Test → click Download PDF at the end.
  3. Do the Fill in the Blanks → click Download PDF at the end.
  4. Finish the Speaking Prompts → click Download PDF for Preply.
  5. Send all 3 PDFs to me on Preply before our Wednesday lesson.

How to upload your PDF to Preply

  1. Open preply.com and log in.
  2. Go to Messages and open the chat with your teacher.
  3. Click the paperclip / attachment icon in the message box.
  4. Choose the PDF from your Downloads folder (e.g. paolo-idiom-quiz-2026-05-20.pdf).
  5. Add a short message like “Homework for Wednesday” and press Send.

Tip: on a phone, the paperclip might say “Attach file” — choose File / Document and find the PDF in your Downloads.
